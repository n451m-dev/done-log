Welcome to DoneLog

DoneLog is a Chrome extension that helps you stay productive by managing your daily tasks efficiently.

Features include:

  • Motivational quotes displayed in the top bar.
  • Personalized greetings based on your local time.
  • Task management with start and end times.
  • Automatically push completed tasks to your Google Sheets.
  • Seamless deletion of completed tasks every day.

Start using DoneLog today and boost your productivity effortlessly!
